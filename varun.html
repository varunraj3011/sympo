A National Level Technical Symposium

Epsilon2k25 is a national-level technical symposium organized by the Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) Department of Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College. This annual event brings together talented students from across the country to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and innovation. It features a variety of technical and non-technical events, workshops, offering participants a chance to compete, learn, and network with industry experts.
